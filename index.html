FirstBud

NodeJS Application

FirstBud is a NodeJS Web Application made specifically for new SEGi Students who might need help navigating within the campus. The application shows an indoor map of the university with certain key places acting as starting points. It generates directions based on the user's current location and destination. In addition to the map, FirstBud also helps students find restaurants based on their cuisine of choice and nearby amenities like bus/train stations, shopping malls and the like. Amenities and restaurants are recommended to all users based on the number of 'Likes' they receive from the user base. The application was developed as part of my Degree Final Year Project.

Travel Agency

PHP Application

Le'Mone is a Travel Agency Portal made with PHP. Users can visit the site to view and book travel packages and flights. In addition, a content management system is in place to view total number of travel packages booked by the site visitors and manage the registered users of the site. This was developed as part of a my Degree PHP module coursework.
